INTERNAL MEMORANDUM – TRUMP ORGANIZATION
TO:      D. Trump
FROM:    General Counsel
DATE:    July 22, 2002
RE:      Epstein relationship – legal assessment

Following your request, we have reviewed the nature of the
organisation's association with Mr. Jeffrey Epstein.

SUMMARY OF CONTACTS:
- Mar-a-Lago: Epstein held a membership (1991-2004). Membership
  subsequently revoked following 2004 allegations.
- Joint events: Approximately 8 documented joint social appearances
  (1993-2002)
- Shared associates: Bill Clinton, Alan Dershowitz, Naomi Campbell,
  Prince Andrew (indirect), Les Wexner, Harvey Weinstein (one occasion)

RECOMMENDATION:
Given the press inquiry from the Palm Beach Post, we recommend
formally distancing the organisation from Mr. Epstein.
No further joint appearances. Revoke club membership.
Refer all media inquiries to external counsel.

This memo is attorney-client privileged.
