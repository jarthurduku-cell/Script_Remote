PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Donald Trump]

December 29, 1999

New Year's at Mar-a-Lago with Donald. He is exhausting and
magnificent in equal measure. The room loves him.

He pulled me aside near midnight and said, with genuine earnestness,
that I was the most interesting man in Palm Beach. Coming from
Donald that is either a great compliment or a warning. Possibly both.

Bill Clinton called to wish me happy new year just after midnight.
Donald was standing right next to me and I watched something
flicker across his face. Complicated feelings there.

August 14, 2000

Dinner at Mar-a-Lago. Donald in tremendous form. Naomi Campbell
arrived late and Donald's attention shifted entirely, as it always
does when a beautiful woman enters a room.

Leonardo DiCaprio was at the bar with a group of friends.
Donald knew him slightly and brought him over. DiCaprio was
polite, slightly guarded, clearly assessing the room.
