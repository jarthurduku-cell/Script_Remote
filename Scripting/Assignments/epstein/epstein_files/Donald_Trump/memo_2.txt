INTERNAL MEMORANDUM – TRUMP ORGANIZATION
TO:      D. Trump
FROM:    Communications Director
DATE:    March 14, 2005
RE:      Media strategy – Epstein press coverage

Following the Palm Beach Post coverage and subsequent wire stories,
we have been contacted by the following outlets:

- New York Post (Rupert Murdoch – call handled, referred to counsel)
- Page Six (request for comment – declined)
- CNN (interview request – declined)
- ABC News (interview request – declined)

Our position remains: Mr. Trump had a social acquaintance with
Mr. Epstein consistent with Palm Beach social circles. The membership
was revoked proactively in 2004. No further comment.

Alan Dershowitz's office has been copied on this memo.
Bill Clinton's team have separately issued a similar statement.
Prince Andrew's office has not yet commented publicly.
