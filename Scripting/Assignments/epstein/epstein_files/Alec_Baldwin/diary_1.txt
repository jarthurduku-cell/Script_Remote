PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Alec Baldwin]

March 16, 2001

Alec Baldwin at the gala. He is funnier than his public persona
suggests and considerably less angry. He and Donald Trump managed
to avoid each other with what I suspect was considerable mutual effort.

He asked about the island. I was noncommittal.

Robert De Niro was also there. He is extraordinary to watch
in a social setting – completely still, completely present,
missing nothing. He and Alec spoke for a long time.
I have no idea what they discussed.

Harvey Weinstein arrived late and the atmosphere in the room
shifted immediately. That man carries a weather system with him.
