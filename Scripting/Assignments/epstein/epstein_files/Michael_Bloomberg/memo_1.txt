INTERNAL MEMORANDUM – BLOOMBERG LP
TO:      M. Bloomberg
FROM:    Chief of Staff
DATE:    November 5, 2003
RE:      Epstein dinner – debrief

The dinner at the townhouse on November 3rd was attended by
approximately twenty guests including Alan Dershowitz,
Tony Blair, Larry Summers, and Al Gore.

Mr. Epstein raised the question of philanthropic collaboration.
You indicated interest in the education initiative.
No commitments were made.

Rupert Murdoch sent his apologies for the dinner but called the
following morning. The nature of that call should be noted:
he was asking questions, not sharing information.

Recommendation: Maintain cordial distance. The Florida situation
is becoming a press story. Reduce visibility of association.
