PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Prince Andrew]

July 15, 2001

Andrew arrived from London looking tired but brightened immediately
when he saw Bill Clinton on the terrace. Those two have a remarkable
friendship. Bill produces this effect on almost everyone but with
Andrew there is something genuine underneath it – a mutual recognition,
I think, of what it costs to be born into an impossible role.

Kevin Spacey and Chris Tucker arrived together. The house immediately
became louder and warmer. Naomi held court at dinner.

I looked around the table and thought: no one outside this island
would believe this room exists.

July 17, 2001

Bill left this morning. Andrew was visibly subdued afterward.
He and I took a long walk along the north beach and he spoke
more openly than I have ever heard him speak. About his family.
About what is expected. About what he actually wants.

I said nothing. Sometimes the most useful thing you can offer
a person is silence.
