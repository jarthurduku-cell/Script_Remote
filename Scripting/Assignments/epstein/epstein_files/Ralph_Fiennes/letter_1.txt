                                          Ralph Fiennes
                                          London

                                          September 3, 2003

Dear Jeffrey,

Thank you for a most unusual few days.

The island is unlike anywhere I have ever been – not in its
physical beauty, though that is considerable, but in its
atmosphere. There is something at once rarefied and slightly
unreal about it.

Kevin Spacey sends his regards. We had dinner in London last week
and spoke of you warmly.

I am not sure I will return, but I am glad to have been there once.

Yours sincerely,
Ralph Fiennes
