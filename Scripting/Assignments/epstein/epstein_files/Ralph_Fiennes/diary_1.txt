PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Ralph Fiennes]

August 12, 2003

Ralph arrived quietly, as he always does. He and Kevin Spacey
spent most of the first evening in deep conversation about Olivier.

Prince Andrew joined us for dinner and was particularly taken
with Ralph's description of filming in Eastern Europe.

Ralph asked me at one point what exactly it was I did. I gave
my usual answer. He nodded in that way that suggests he does
not entirely believe you but is too polite to say so.

August 14, 2003

Ralph left early. He thanked me warmly. I think he will not
come back. Some people sense things.
