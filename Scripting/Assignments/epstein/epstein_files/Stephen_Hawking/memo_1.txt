INTERNAL MEMORANDUM
TO:      J. Epstein
FROM:    G. Maxwell
DATE:    September 2, 2005
RE:      St. Thomas symposium – debrief

The August symposium was an unqualified success.

Stephen Hawking's presence transformed the event. Bill Gates
flew in specifically for his lecture and stayed an extra day as
a result. Bill Clinton was visibly moved during the evening session
on cosmology and spent the following morning in extended conversation
with Stephen on the terrace.

Larry Summers managed to arrive a day late, which was on balance
a net positive for the atmosphere.

Ehud Barak attended the full week and was excellent company.

Recommendation: repeat annually. Stephen has indicated willingness
to attend again provided accessibility arrangements are identical.
