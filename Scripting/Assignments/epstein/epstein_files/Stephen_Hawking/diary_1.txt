PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Stephen Hawking]

August 7, 2005

Stephen arrived with two members of his care team and was installed
in the downstairs suite. He seemed comfortable immediately.

The dinner that evening was everything I had hoped. Bill Clinton
sat to Stephen's left and was visibly transfixed throughout.
Prince Andrew made a remark about cosmology that was rather better
than I expected and Stephen's synthesiser produced what I believe
was a laugh.

August 8, 2005

Stephen gave his informal lecture on the terrace after breakfast.
Bill Gates had flown in overnight – he sat in the front row
and barely moved for ninety minutes.

I looked around that terrace and thought: I will never be able
to assemble a room quite like this again.
