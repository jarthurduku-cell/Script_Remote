PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Courtney Love]

October 10, 2003

Received a curious email from Courtney Love via Ghislaine.
She claims to know things. She may well know things. She moves
in wide circles and people talk.

Alan Dershowitz's view: do nothing. She is not credible to
the press. He is probably right. He is usually right.

Still. It unsettled me.

Donald Trump called in the afternoon, unrelated. He wanted to
discuss Palm Beach real estate. The juxtaposition was jarring.

Harvey Weinstein also called. He and Courtney Love apparently
have history. I wonder if that is how she knows what she knows.
