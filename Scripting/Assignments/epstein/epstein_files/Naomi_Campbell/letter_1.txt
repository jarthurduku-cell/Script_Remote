                                          Naomi Campbell
                                          London

                                          August 30, 2001

Dear Jeffrey,

I am writing from London where I have just arrived after the
most extraordinary summer. Thank you from the bottom of my heart.

The week on the island was everything – the people, the warmth,
the extraordinary conversations. I have never felt so completely
among my own kind, if that makes any sense.

Prince Andrew was magnificent as always. He called me yesterday
from Balmoral. Kevin Spacey and Chris Tucker made every single
evening feel like a celebration.

I want to come back. Tell me there will be a next time.

With all my love,
Naomi
