PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Naomi Campbell file]

March 8, 2003

Naomi arrived from Milan looking extraordinary as always.
She brought Mick Jagger along uninvited. Mick and Bill Clinton
had a long and apparently hilarious conversation about Arkansas.

Naomi pulled me aside in the evening and asked about Prince Andrew.
I told her he would not be joining this particular trip.
She seemed disappointed.

March 10, 2003

Small dinner – just Naomi, Chris Tucker, Kevin Spacey and myself.
One of those rare evenings where everything is perfect.
Chris was in extraordinary form. Naomi laughed until she cried.

Jay-Z called Naomi during dinner. She stepped outside for ten minutes.
When she came back she seemed distracted. I did not ask.
