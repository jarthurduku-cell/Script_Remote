PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Mick Jagger]

March 8, 2003

Mick Jagger arrived unannounced. He and Naomi have history
that fills every room they share with a particular electricity.

Mick and Bill Clinton ended up at the end of the terrace for
nearly two hours. I have absolutely no idea what they talked about.
Both of them seemed enormously pleased with themselves afterward.

Prince Andrew watched the whole thing from across the room with
an expression of pure entertainment.

Sting apparently called Mick that evening wanting to know where
he was. Mick told him nothing. Good instinct.
