Dear Jeffrey,

Thank you for the most wonderful week on the island. I have
been back in London for three weeks now and still find myself
thinking of those long dinners on the terrace.

Ralph Fiennes called me last week and said much the same.
He was particularly taken with the conversation about astrophysics
that Stephen Hawking led on the Thursday evening.

I would very much like to come back next summer if the invitation
stands. Chris Tucker has already called me twice asking if I have
heard from you about dates.

With gratitude and warm regards,
Kevin
