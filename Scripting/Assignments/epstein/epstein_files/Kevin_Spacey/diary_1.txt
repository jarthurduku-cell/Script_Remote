PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries]

July 17, 2001

Kevin arrived with Chris Tucker. Those two together fill a room.
Naomi arrived an hour later and the evening just took off.

Prince Andrew was in excellent humour. He and Kevin got along
famously – long conversation about theatre, the monarchy,
American versus British acting traditions.

Bill Clinton arrived late and the room shifted again, as it
always does when he enters.

July 19, 2001

Quieter day. Kevin and Chris went snorkelling in the morning.
I had a long walk with Bill along the beach.

August 16, 2004

Kevin back again. He seems different from 2001 – more guarded,
more watchful. He and Ralph Fiennes sat on the terrace for hours
talking in low voices. I did not interrupt.

I mentioned Harvey Weinstein had been in touch through a mutual
contact. Kevin went very still. He said: do not invite him here.
I told him I had no intention of doing so. He seemed relieved.
