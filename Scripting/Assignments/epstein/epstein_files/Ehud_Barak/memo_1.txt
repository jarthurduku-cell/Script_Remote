INTERNAL MEMORANDUM
TO:      J. Epstein
FROM:    G. Maxwell
DATE:    April 16, 2004
RE:      April New York dinner – debrief

The dinner on April 13th was productive.

Ehud Barak and Bill Clinton had an extended private conversation
after dinner that I understand went well. Henry Kissinger joined
later than expected but his presence immediately elevated the
seriousness of the room. He and Ehud together are formidable –
there is a weight to that combination that is almost physical.

Alan Dershowitz managed the conversation well and kept things
moving when Kissinger began what threatened to be a very long
story about Nixon.

Bill Gates arrived late but was in excellent form.

Overall: very successful.
