                                          Dustin Hoffman
                                          Los Angeles, CA

                                          June 18, 2002

Dear Jeffrey,

Thank you for the dinner last month. I am sorry I had to leave early.

Woody Allen tells me I missed an extraordinary evening. He said
Larry Summers argued with Alan Dershowitz for an hour about something
legal that devolved into something philosophical and then somehow
became a discussion of Chekhov. Only in New York.

Harvey Weinstein apparently arrived after I left. Woody said
the atmosphere changed the moment he walked in. I cannot say
I am sorry I missed that.

Warmly,
Dustin
