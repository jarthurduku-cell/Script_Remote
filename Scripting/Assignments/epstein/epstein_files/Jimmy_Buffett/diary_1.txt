PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – Jimmy Buffett]

January 1, 2002

Jimmy Buffett sailed over from St. Barts on the afternoon of the 31st
and the whole atmosphere of the island immediately shifted.

Bill Clinton was delighted to see him. Naomi arrived an hour
after Jimmy and the New Year's dinner became something else entirely.

Jimmy played guitar on the terrace after midnight for about two hours.
Prince Andrew sat in the front row and did not move.

Some evenings you know, even as they are happening, that you will
not see their like again.
