                                          Woody Allen
                                          New York, NY

                                          May 3, 2002

Dear Jeffrey,

Thank you for the dinner last Tuesday. I am always happy to
be somewhere that does not involve talking about film.

Your friend Larry Summers is extremely clever and exhausting
in equal measure. I like him.

Dustin Hoffman was there briefly and sends his regards.
He had to leave early – something about a screening.

Harvey Weinstein was also there for a time. I noticed you
kept him at the far end of the table from Kevin Spacey,
which I thought was considerate.

New York is small enough that everyone knows everyone eventually.
I have learned to make peace with that.

Yours,
Woody
