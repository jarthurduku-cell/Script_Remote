PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries – David Copperfield]

April 4, 2001

Dinner with David Copperfield at the townhouse. He was in
excellent form – performed a few close-up illusions after dinner
that left even Ghislaine genuinely baffled. He and Naomi Campbell
apparently know each other well from Monte Carlo.

Mentioned the island. He seemed interested.

April 22, 2001

David confirmed he will join us in July. He and Kevin Spacey have
apparently crossed paths before at some Hollywood event.
