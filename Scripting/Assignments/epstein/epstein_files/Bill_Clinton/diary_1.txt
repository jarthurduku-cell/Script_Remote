PERSONAL DIARY – J.E.
[Selected entries]

February 15, 2002

Arrived Nassau late morning. Bill was in excellent form – relaxed,
funny, completely at ease as always. Prince Andrew joined us for
dinner; those two together are remarkable to watch. Chris Tucker kept
the whole table in stitches until well past midnight.

February 16, 2002

Flew to the island with Bill, Andrew and Chris. Quiet day.
Bill spent much of the afternoon reading on the terrace.
We talked at length about the foundation work – he has grand plans.
Mentioned Al Gore called him last week about the climate initiative.

November 9, 2003

Miami stopover with Bill. Ehud called ahead to confirm he would
meet us in St. Thomas. Good mood all around.

August 5, 2005

Perhaps the finest gathering I have ever arranged. Bill and Prince Andrew
on the terrace after dinner with Stephen Hawking holding court on
the nature of time. Ehud Barak listening with that particular intensity
he has. Larry Summers arrived a day late and immediately tried to steer
the conversation toward economics. Bill shot me a look across the table.

Henry Kissinger called in the afternoon – he had heard the gathering
was happening and wanted to know if it was too late to join.
I told him it was. He accepted this with his customary grace,
which is to say not entirely gracefully.
