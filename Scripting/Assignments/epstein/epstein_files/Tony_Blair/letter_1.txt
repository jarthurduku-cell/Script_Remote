                                          Tony Blair
                                          Office of Tony Blair
                                          London

                                          June 12, 2006

Mr. Jeffrey Epstein
9 East 71st Street
New York, NY 10021

Dear Jeffrey,

I wanted to write personally to thank you for the extraordinary
dinner last month. The quality of conversation – with Bill Clinton,
Larry Summers, Michael Bloomberg and the others – reminded me
of what I miss most about being at the centre of things.

Bill Clinton and I have spoken twice since and are making real
progress on the Africa initiative. Your network and your generosity
in facilitating these connections is genuinely remarkable.

Prince Andrew sends his regards.

With gratitude,
Tony Blair
